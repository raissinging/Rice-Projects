Who am I?

My name is Jeffrey Zhong and I am currently a first-generation student at Rice University (’24) intending on double majoring in Computer Science and Statistics. I was born and raised in Southern California and attended The Webb Schools of California (high school) where I graduated with cum laude honors. While I was initially intending to become a pharmaceutical scientist, classes I took in high school, such as my dual enrollment classes at Harvey Mudd College, inspired me to pursue a different path: one in cs and stats. As a college student, I continue to explore my interests and push myself as much as I can to become the best person I can be. I hope and am excited to make a good and meaningful impact in the world by sharing and pursuing my passions.
